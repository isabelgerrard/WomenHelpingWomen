How to Help


Women's Daytime Drop-In Center
https://www.womensdropin.org/
The Women's Daytime Drop-In Center gives homeless families a home when there isn't one, resources to identify housing, parenting support, and provides women's empowerment. Their site has online tutorials for homeless women struggling with childcare as well as information and volunteer opportunities for people who just want to help!

Hand Up
https://handup.org/
Hand Up is an organization dedicated to providing the homeless with whatever they may need but are not able to afford. The site sets up profiles of people requesting a certain item or money for a service and their reasoning behind this request. Hand up creates a user-friendly environment that allows donors to connect personally with dontatees.

Boss
https://self-sufficiency.org/
The mission of BOSS is to help homeless, poor, and disabled people achieve health and self-sufficiency, and to fight against the root causes of poverty and homelessness. The organization was founded in 1971 by a group of volunteers from the Hillel Streetwork Project in Berkeley who responded to the needs of mentally ill individuals being released to the streets by state hospital closures. Volunteers provided street outreach, crisis intervention, and benefits advocacy to the emerging homeless population.

The National Alliance to End Homelessness
https://endhomelessness.org/help-end-homelessness/support-our-work/
The National Alliance to End Homelessness is a leading voice on federal homelessness policy. It analyzes enacted and proposed legislation and advocates for improved federal policy. The Alliance analyzes data and research so that policymakers, practitioners, and the public have the best information about trends in homelessness and emerging solutions. It also provides capacity-building assistance to help communities turn policy solutions and proven best practices into viable, on-the-ground programs.
